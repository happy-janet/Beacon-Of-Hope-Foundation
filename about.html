About Us

We are Vision Mines & Trading Company LTD a registered Certified Mineral Company in Uganda under the Companies Act with a Registration Certificate Number 224860. We are a leading Ugandan diversified mining and minerals company with long-life, low unit cost operations and significant growth opportunities. Vision Mines & Trading Company LTD brings carefully balanced combination of knowledge of the local conditions, the innovative approach to mining, and the experience in work in different partnerships, enhance Vision Mines & Trading Company LTD's capabilities to effectively manage perceptions of key operations and expectise in the sales, marketing and creative financing structures that will provide funds to secure equipments and logistics to move the product from its raw forms into a value added commodity for international standard . Adherence to stringent ethical standards, as well as the use of creative mining tools make us an extra ordinary mining company in Uganda. At Vision Mines & Trading Company LTD, we understand the significant impact that our company has on the lives of people, the environment and the communities that surround our mines, and the economic strength of the countries in which we operate. We measure up and we deliver. Vision Mines & Trading Company LTD will take up corporate responsibilty to mine in a smart environment that reclaims the land and continuously make it green while protecting the environment.

Mining

Identifying and assessing mineral reserves in different geographical areas. Extracting minerals from mines using different techniques like open-pit mining or underground mining.

Trading

We sell both large and small quantities of gold, diamond and tanzanite to manufacturers, investors, and to other clients. We offer an online platform for buying these minerals in various forms.

Transportation

Handling the logistics, paperwork, and regulations involved in importing and exporting the minerals across borders.
